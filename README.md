# 🚶 Step Counter & Reward Mobile Application

![Project Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Design%20Phase-yellow)
![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/Platform-Mobile%20App-blue)
![SDGs](https://img.shields.io/badge/SDGs-Good%20Health%20%7C%20Sustainable%20Cities-green)

A mobile application designed to promote physical activity and walking by offering a token-based reward system. This project aims to encourage users to walk more, reduce short-distance vehicle usage, and contribute to reducing air pollution, aligning with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs 3: Good Health and Well-being, and SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities).

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

1. **Promote Health:** Develop a mobile application that encourages people to exercise through daily walking.
2. **Reward Motivation:** Motivate users to walk more by rewarding them with Tokens that can be exchanged for prizes within the app.
3. **Sustainable Environment:** Reduce short-distance vehicle usage to help lower air pollution and promote an eco-friendly lifestyle.

---

## 👥 Target Audience

The primary target audience includes students and young working professionals aged **18–35 years old** who frequently use smartphones and need the motivation to exercise through daily walking routines.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🏠 **Home Page / Dashboard:** Overview of daily steps, collected Tokens, and activity summary.
- 📊 **Step Statistics (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Yearly):** Track walking history and monitor progress over time.
- 🎯 **Goal Setting:** Set personalized daily step goals (e.g., 5,000 or 10,000 steps).
- 🏆 **Daily Challenges:** Complete specific walking missions within a time frame to earn extra Tokens.
- 🔥 **Streak System:** Encourages consistency by tracking consecutive days of reaching step goals.
- 🏅 **Leaderboard:** Compete with other users to rank highest in step counts, fostering a healthy competitive environment.
- 🎁 **Rewards:** Exchange accumulated Tokens for real-world or digital prizes.
- 🗺️ **Route Map:** Map feature to record and view walking routes.
- ⌚ **Health App Integration:** Syncs seamlessly with Smartwatches and default Health apps.
- 📤 **Social Share:** Share daily progress, burned calories, and routes to social media platforms (e.g., Instagram).
